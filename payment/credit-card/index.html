Credit card payments

Are my credit cards safe with Testomato?​

Yes. We are using BraintreePayments gateway for processing and storing your credit card information.

Braintree Payments is a merchant account provider based in Chicago, Illinois. Founded in 2007, Braintree markets its services primarily to e-commerce merchants, subscription-based businesses, and merchants that key in all of their credit card transactions. About Braintree
